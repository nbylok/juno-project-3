About Us

At Bylok & Associates, we specialize in solutions-based research to provide stakeholders with the insights needed to power great decisions. We work with trusted associates on a per project basis, to ensure your needs are met and delivered upon.

About John Bylok

For over 25 years, John Bylok has provided expertise and insights in pharma and consumer marketing, communication, business development and research to clients. He was a director of market research for Gallup Canada. He’s been product manager for 12 brands including Paxil, Wellbutrin, Coreg, Valtrex, Ceftin and Bactroban – and he’s served as the Business Development Director at MediResource.
